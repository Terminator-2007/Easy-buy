📏 Size Guide

Find your perfect fit. All measurements are in centimetres (cm) unless stated.

📐
Chest

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape level.

📏
Shoulder

Measure across the back from shoulder point to shoulder point.

↕️
Length

From the highest point of the shoulder straight down to the desired length.

🏷️ Slim Fit

Cut close to the body. If you're between sizes, we recommend going one size up for comfort. Works best for lean to athletic builds.

🏷️ Regular Fit

A relaxed fit with room to move. True to size. Most of our casual shirts and everyday tees use this fit.

🏷️ Oversized Fit

Intentionally roomy. For the intended look, go with your regular size. Sizing down gives a more regular silhouette.

🏷️ Linen / Woven Shirts

Linen relaxes slightly after washing. We recommend your true size — they won't shrink significantly if washed in cold water.

📐
Waist

Measure around your natural waistline, above the hip bone. Keep a finger under the tape for comfort fit.

📏
Hip

Measure around the fullest part of your hips, approximately 20 cm below the waistline.

↕️
Inseam

From the crotch seam straight down to the ankle. Use a pair of well-fitting trousers as a guide.

Measure Your Foot
