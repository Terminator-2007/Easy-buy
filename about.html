Our Story

Easy Buy started in a small apartment in Bengaluru in 2021. Three friends — frustrated by the gap between aspirational fashion and what most people could actually afford — decided to build something different. No inflated markups. No confusing size charts that didn't apply to Indian body types. No slow delivery that made online shopping feel unreliable.

Today, Easy Buy ships over 50,000 orders a month across India. We've grown from three people to over 200, but our core mission hasn't changed: make fashion accessible to everyone, everywhere.
